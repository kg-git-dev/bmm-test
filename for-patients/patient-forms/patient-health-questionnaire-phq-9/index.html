Interpretation of final score:
0-4 none, 5-9 mild, 10-14 moderate, 15-19 moderately severe, 20-27 severe.

If you are experiencing moderate to severe depression, it is essential to get professional help. Getting help is important as depression is not something that will just go away. This is a chronic condition, and it can be treated effectively.
