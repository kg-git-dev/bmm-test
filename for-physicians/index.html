Referring a Patient to Beautiful Minds

If you have a patient who is interested in a holistic approach to mental health, Beautiful Minds is here to help.

We specialize in evidence-based mental healthcare with an added emphasis on lifestyle medicine. We provide outpatient psychiatry, counseling, and intensive outpatient care. We also specialize in the difficult to diagnose and treat patient.

Questions:

  • Is the patient interested in mental health treatment that goes beyond (but does not exclude) standard treatment with medications? (i.e. mental health from a lifestyle perspective)
  • Has the patient been diagnosed with depression or anxiety and is refractory to treatment in the primary care setting?
  • Does the patient possibly have bipolar disorder, a psychotic disorder, or a combination of substance use and a mental health disorder?
  • Does the patient have a mental health disorder that has been difficult to diagnose?

If you answered “yes” to any of the above questions, please refer your patient to Beautiful Minds.
